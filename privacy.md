Heatbit (“the App”) is developed by an independent creator. This Privacy Policy explains how your data is handled when you use the App.

1. Information Heatbit Does NOT Collect

Heatbit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
Specifically:

❌ No account creation

❌ No analytics or tracking

❌ No advertising identifiers

❌ No third-party SDKs

❌ No cloud storage

❌ No location data

❌ No contact information

Everything stays on your device only.

2. Data Stored On Your Device

Heatbit stores habit names, habit colors, and your check-ins locally on your device’s storage using Apple’s standard data containers.

This data:

remains on your device

is never transmitted to any server

is deleted only when you uninstall the app or use the “Reset All Data” option

3. In-App Purchases

Heatbit uses Apple’s secure StoreKit system for purchases.
Apple may process payment information, but Heatbit receives no access to your name, email, payment method, or billing details.

4. App Permissions

Heatbit does not request or require any device permissions.

5. Children’s Privacy

Heatbit does not target children and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy changes, the updated version will be posted on the official support page.
