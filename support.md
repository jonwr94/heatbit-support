FAQ
• How do I restore my purchase?

Tap Settings → Heatbit Pro → Restore Purchases.

• My habits are gone after reinstalling

Heatbit stores everything locally. If the app was deleted or reset, your data is cleared.
Export JSON regularly if you want a backup.

• Does Heatbit track me?

No. Heatbit has zero analytics, zero tracking, and stores all data on-device.
